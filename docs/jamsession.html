ISMIR 2015 Pandora Jam Session

The central topic of the ISMIR conference is MUSIC. Therefore, let's PLAY SOME MUSIC!

ISMIR 2015 will feature an amazing Jam Session after the Gala Dinner thanks to the Pandora Sponsorship.

The available instruments will be:

  • COMPLETE DRUM KIT

  • ROLAND RD700 KEYBOARD

  • 1 FENDER STRATOCASTER ELECTRIC GUITAR

  • 1 FENDER TELECASTER ELECTRIC GUITAR

  • 1 ELECTRIC BASS

  • 1 ACOUSTIC GUITAR

  • 1 CAJÓN FLAMENCO

  • 1 SPANISH GUITAR

There will be also microphones available for those who want to sign.

HERE you can download a book with different songs (The Beatles, Metallica, AC/DC, Queen...), so that you can get some inspiration about what to play... but you are welcome to play any other musical piece.
