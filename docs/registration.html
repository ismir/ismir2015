Registration

ONLY ONSITE REGISTRATION AVAILABLE NOW!

The registration service is provided by lamardeventos. Should you have any questions about the registration, please contact them at secretaria1@lamardeventos.com

If you want tips about the accomodation, have a look at the ISMIR2015 accomodation web page.

Your ISMIR 2015 registration includes:

  • Your individual 2015-2016 ISMIR membership fee (i.e. 10€)

  • Attendance to all ISMIR 2015 paper sessions, posters, invited speeches between 27th-30th October 2015

  • ISMIR 2015 Coffee breaks

  • ISMIR 2015 lunch tickets

  • 1 ticket for the ISMIR 2015 Welcome Reception on 26th October

  • Attendance to the ISMIR 2015 Concert on 28th October

  • 1 ticket for the ISMIR 2015 Gala Dinner on 29th October

  • An electronic version of the ISMIR 2015 Conference Proceedings

Important:

  • Remember that, for each paper/music piece, at least one author must be registered for the conference by July 16, 2015. If none of the authors is registered by this date, the paper/music piece will be removed from the conference proceedings and programme.

  • One regular registration covers at most two submissions (The author registered must be an author of both contributions).

  • One student registration covers at most one submission (The student registered must be an author of the contribution). You must provide proof of your student status and your advisor's information (including name and email) to get the student discount. Please send it to secretaria1@lamardeventos.com(post-doc are not eligible for student rates).

Invitation letter:

If you need an invitation letter, after completing your registration and payment, send a request by e-mail to secretaria1@lamardeventos.com
