Satellite event: HAMR@ISMIR 2015

Hacking Audio and Music Research HAMR@ISMIR 2015 will be held October 24 - 25th, 2015 in conjuntion with ISMIR 2015.

HAMR is a hackathon series focusing on the development of new techniques for analyzing, processing, and synthesizing audio and music signals.

The venue for HAMR@ISMIR 2015 will be the The Green Ray building of the Universidad de Málaga. For further information about the HAMR@ISMIR2015 venue go HERE
